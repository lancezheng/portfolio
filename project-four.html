Extreme Heat

  • My Role: Graphic design, Website design

  • Date: September - December 2017

  • Type of project: Academic project

  • Group Member: Erica Zhang , Sherry Liao, Lance Zheng

Description of the project

The goal of this project is to design the website and offline approach for helping City of Vancouver residents in managing extreme heat during the summer months.

Project Process

Generated Sitemap and Wireframe - Built Website- Designed Offline Approach

Generated Sitemap and Wireframe

I started from the sitemap to have a entirety of vision for the site. According to sitemap’s structure, I built a wireframe using Axure with responsive rule in mind.

Open Wireframe

Built the website

Based on the information that was provided by the instructor , I made an extension of assets and texts that can help us build the website. The main color scheme is blue so that residents can feel a relief from extreme heat weather. I decided to pick the concept of “freeze your heat” as an interesting way to help residents of Vancouver to understand what is extreme heat and how to deal with extreme heat. The website is straight-forward and the category is easy to use for wide range of target customers.

Open Website

Designed Offline Approach

Based on our concept, we design a water machine which can be put in outdoor for the residents who want ice water. The goal of chat box on the right side is to lead resident to check our website.

Retrospective

This project allowed me to utilize a breadth of skills that I have developed in the class. The range of target customer is broad so our team has a conflict with the theme but we learned to solve the different opinion and find a balance between us. We had a showcase in the city of Vancouver and we received a lot of favourable and unfavourable feedbacks. I learned from the valuable feedbacks and iterated the website.
